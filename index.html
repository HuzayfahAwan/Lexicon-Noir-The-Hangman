DIFFICULTY

Medium-length words. 6 lives.

Rules

1. A secret word is chosen from your selected category.

2. Each dash represents one letter in the word.

3. Guess one letter at a time — type it or click the on-screen keyboard.

4. A wrong guess draws a body part on the gallows. Too many wrong guesses = game over.

5. Press H or click USE HINT to reveal a random letter — costs 1 life.

6. Daily Challenge: one unique word each day, same for everyone.

7. Timed: 90 second clock. Wrong guesses cost extra seconds.

8. Two Player: Player 1 types a secret word, Player 2 guesses it.
